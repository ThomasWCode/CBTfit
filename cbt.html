Cognitive Behavioural Therapy Explained

description - something random I generated with AI as a palceholder:

CBT is a collaborative, practical approach that focuses on the connection between your thoughts, feelings, and behaviours. It helps you identify and challenge unhelpful thought patterns that contribute to emotional distress.

Through structured sessions, you'll learn practical skills to manage your difficulties. CBT is evidence-based, time-limited, and goal-oriented, making it highly effective for a range of mental health issues.

The approach is active and participatory — you'll be encouraged to practise techniques between sessions and apply them to real-life situations.

The CBTfit Approach

description - something random I generated with AI as a palceholder:

At CBTfit, we go beyond traditional CBT by integrating mind and body wellness into our therapeutic approach. We believe that mental health cannot be fully addressed without considering the physical self.

Our holistic method incorporates:

  • Core CBT techniques — thought records, behavioural experiments, and cognitive restructuring
  • Mindfulness and body-awareness practices
  • Breathing and relaxation strategies
  • Physical activity and movement guidance
  • Lifestyle factors — sleep, nutrition, and routine

This integrated approach helps you build resilience from the inside out, creating sustainable change that lasts.
